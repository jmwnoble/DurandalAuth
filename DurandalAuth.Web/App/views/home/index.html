Here's how it works...

Meet CueZero...

CueZero is always on, managing feedback in real time and pivoting towards new trends as they arise.

A live dashboard highlights the key questions facing your business at any given moment and is always open for new responses.

Follow or escalate trends as they evolve, adding or updating new questions as you go so that you’re able to follow the feedback trail.

Meet CueZero...

CueZero encourages active, ongoing participation.

Staff can view their responses in context, understand differing perspectives and build upon existing feedback and ideas, while confidentiality settings give you the control you need. Leaders and staff can post follow-up questions to uncover root causes and move from identifying problems and opportunities to generating potential solutions.

Receive, explore and understand feedback all in one place, as part of the one process.

Meet CueZero...

CueZero is a two-way feedback channel.

It provides an efficient, targeted mechanism for you to acknowledge feedback, post updates, and recognise the impact of contributions. It’s also a way to manage expectations and reinforce the strategic context.

Make leadership communication an integrated part of the feedback and improvement cycle rather than a disconnected process.
